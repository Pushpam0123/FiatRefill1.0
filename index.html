What is FiatRefill?

FiatRefill is your bridge to the world of Indian e-commerce, powered by cryptocurrency. We enable you to shop from any online store like Amazon India or Flipkart, even if they don't accept crypto directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this service work?

It's simple! You paste the link of the product you want, provide your delivery details, and we send you a crypto payment link. Once you pay, we order the product to your address. You get to use your crypto, and we handle the fiat conversion and purchase.

Which cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We accept a wide range of cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), and other major altcoins. The final payment link will show all available options.

Are there any hidden fees?

We charge a small service fee on top of the product price and any applicable crypto network fees. The complete breakdown will be provided with your payment link, ensuring full transparency before you pay.
